Nirmaan Organization is a registered NGO,
started by students of BITS Pilani in 2005.
We work in the areas of Education,
Skill Development & Entrepreneurship, Health and Wellbeing, Community Development
and Social Leadership. In the past 16 years of journey,
we have impacted the lives of 1.5+ million beneficiaries with the support
of 300+ full time employees and 1000+ strong volunteer network,
through various impact-oriented Flagship Programs & Social Leadership Initiatives
benefiting Children, Women, Youth, Persons with Disabilities (PwDs), LGBTQ+
and Farmers from diverse social backgrounds across 15 States of India
while partnering with 100+ Corporate, Government and Philanthropic partners.
